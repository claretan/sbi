BEHAVIOURAL SCIENCE, Computational techniques, ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS

We are a multi-disciplinary resarch group that combines social-behavioural science with computational insight to identify actionable causes of behaviour.

Areas of Expertise

  • PSYCHOLOGICAL MEASUREMENT: self-report instruments, psychometrics, choice tasks, focus groups
  • BEHAVRIOUAL EXPERIMENTATION: randomised tests, economic games, immersive experiences, quasi-experiments
  • SOCIAL PSYCHOLOGY: attitudes, self and groups, preferences, behaviour change
  • COMPUTATIONAL METHODS: graph and networks, tool development, text processing, predictive models, large language models
